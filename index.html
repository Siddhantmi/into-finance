Jiffy Token Benefits

Benefits Of Adopting Cryptocurrency In Jiffy Stock Platform.

We are providing these benefits not because we want to be competitive with every other trading platform, but because we want every type of seller and business to grow to higher levels.

  • Lower Transaction Cost: The cost of blockchain transactions is noticeably lower than the transaction cost of traditional e-commerce. When a brand's traditional eCommerce platforms pay an average of 3-4% on each transaction, compared to cryptocurrency transactions which are infinitesimally low.
  • Reduces Fraudulent Activities: Cryptocurrency payments are chargeback-proof, that is transactions using cryptocurrency are not subject to chargebacks. This is very helpful to our sellers, as chargebacks drain revenue.
  • Market Expansion: There has been rapid growth in the adoption of cryptocurrency. Cash-like transactions between buyers and sellers, not subject to Banks or government regulation, make it especially attractive for commerce in the underdeveloped marketplace.
  • Secure Transaction Records: Blockchain, is an online ledger that keeps records in a way that is impossible or very difficult to hack.
  • Speed: Via the adoption of cryptocurrency, we aim to give sellers the ability to do transactions at the highest speed. Unlike the traditional system where you have to wait for a few days to confirm transactions which cause a delay in business. Transactions are run within seconds
